Professor Jane Smith

Professor of Law, Example University School of Law

Professor Jane Smith writes and teaches in the areas of mass torts, civil procedure, consumer protection, and access to justice.

About Professor Jane Smith

Professor Jane Smith is a law professor whose work focuses on how legal systems address complex civil disputes, public harms, and procedural fairness. Her scholarship and teaching examine the way courts, litigants, and legal institutions respond to large-scale injury and consumer claims.

Areas of focus

  • Mass torts
  • Civil procedure
  • Consumer protection
  • Access to justice
