David Meldofsky

Founder of Lawsuit Informer · California-licensed attorney (CA Bar No. 263673)

David Meldofsky is a California-licensed attorney and the founder of Lawsuit Informer, an attorney-led educational platform focused on lawsuits, toxic exposure, product liability, and consumer safety issues.

His perspective is shaped by multiple sides of the legal system. He has represented injured clients, worked within the legal intake and referral ecosystem, and seen how potential claims are screened, evaluated, and routed in practice. That experience has given him direct insight into how people enter the lawsuit process, where confusion often begins, and how easily important decisions can be shaped by stress, marketing, or incomplete information.

Through Lawsuit Informer, David aims to make that process easier to understand by helping readers navigate legal topics, lawsuit categories, and next-step questions with greater clarity before deciding what to do next.

About David Meldofsky

David created Lawsuit Informer to help readers better understand how lawsuit claims, legal research, and next-step decisions often work in practice. Many people begin researching lawsuits at stressful moments, when legal language is unfamiliar, the path forward is unclear, and online information may mix education, advertising, and intake prompts in ways that are difficult to untangle.

His background includes representing injured clients as well as practical experience with legal intake, referrals, and case coordination. That broader perspective has shaped the site's approach to legal education. Lawsuit Informer is designed to explain how claims are evaluated, how cases move through the system, and what readers should understand before submitting information or pursuing potential legal options.

Lawsuit Informer is an educational platform, not a law firm, and David's role through the site is to provide general legal education and clearer public-facing guidance rather than individualized legal representation through the website itself.

Editorial Approach

Content on Lawsuit Informer is created to provide general educational information about lawsuits and legal issues in a clear, accessible format. The goal is not just to explain legal concepts, but to help readers better understand the real-world decision points that often arise when they are researching a possible claim under stress.

Articles are written to improve clarity, reduce confusion, and distinguish legal education from legal representation, advertising, or case intake. Learn more about how content is created and reviewed by visiting the site's Editorial Policy.
