David Meldofsky

David Meldofsky is a licensed attorney and the founder of Lawsuit Informer, an educational website focused on helping people understand lawsuits, legal rights, and consumer safety issues related to defective products, toxic exposures, and other legal claims.

About David Meldofsky

David created Lawsuit Informer to make complex legal topics easier to understand. Many people encounter unfamiliar legal terms when researching lawsuits, mass tort litigation, or product liability claims. The goal of Lawsuit Informer is to provide clear educational explanations of how lawsuits work and what people should understand when researching potential legal claims.

The site focuses on explaining topics such as:

  • How lawsuits work
  • Mass tort litigation
  • Class action lawsuits
  • Toxic exposure claims
  • Product liability cases
  • Common legal research questions

Editorial Approach

Content on Lawsuit Informer is written to provide general educational information about legal topics. Articles aim to explain legal concepts clearly and accurately while remaining accessible to readers who may not have a legal background.

You can learn more about how content is created and reviewed by visiting the site's Editorial Policy.
