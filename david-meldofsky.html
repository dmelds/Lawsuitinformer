Why Lawsuit Informer Exists

David created Lawsuit Informer to close a gap he kept seeing from the intake side of the industry: people researching a potential lawsuit were running into dense legal jargon at the exact moment they were trying to make important decisions. The site translates how claims are evaluated, how cases move through the system, and what matters at each stage — in plain English, for readers who are trying to figure out what to do next.

Lawsuit Informer is intentionally educational. Reader-to-firm matching runs on a separate property, Lawsuit Center, so the editorial side stays focused on explaining the law rather than selling intake.
