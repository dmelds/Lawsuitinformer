David Meldofsky

Founder of Lawsuit Informer · Licensed Attorney (California)

David Meldofsky is a licensed attorney in California and the founder of Lawsuit Informer, a platform dedicated to explaining lawsuits, toxic exposure risks, and consumer safety issues.

He has worked within the legal referral and intake ecosystem, collaborating with law firms and supporting the intake, review, and referral of potential claims across multiple litigation areas. This experience has given him direct insight into how cases are screened, developed, and pursued—and where breakdowns often occur for individuals seeking help.

Through Lawsuit Informer, David focuses on making complex legal information more transparent and accessible, with the goal of helping people better understand their rights and options.

Last Updated: March 2026

About David Meldofsky

David created Lawsuit Informer to make complex legal topics easier to understand and more accessible to the public. Many people researching lawsuits, toxic exposures, or product liability claims encounter confusing legal language and unclear information at the very moment they are trying to make important decisions.

His background in legal intake, referrals, and case coordination has shaped the site's approach to legal education. Lawsuit Informer is designed to explain how claims are evaluated, how cases move through the system, and what readers should understand when researching potential legal options.
