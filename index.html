Illnesses and Injuries Linked to Lawsuits

Some lawsuits involve claims that chemical exposure, dangerous products, or environmental contamination may contribute to certain illnesses. Explore common conditions discussed in these cases.

Cancer

Kidney Cancer

Some PFAS contamination lawsuits discuss potential links between exposure and kidney cancer.

Hair Relaxer Cancer

Research has examined links between chemical hair relaxer products and cancer risk.

Roundup Cancer

Some lawsuits examine whether glyphosate exposure may increase the risk of non-Hodgkin's lymphoma.

Ovarian Cancer

Some consumer product lawsuits examine potential links between talcum powder use and ovarian cancer risk.

Neurological Conditions

Parkinson's Disease

Some pesticide lawsuits involve allegations that herbicide exposure may increase the risk of Parkinson's disease.

Environmental Exposure

PFAS Exposure

PFAS contamination lawsuits involve claims that “forever chemicals” in drinking water may increase risk of cancer and other health problems.

AFFF Firefighting Foam

Some lawsuits claim PFAS chemicals in firefighting foam contaminated groundwater near military bases and airports.
