About Lawsuit Informer

Lawsuit Informer is an attorney-led educational platform focused on helping readers better understand lawsuits, consumer safety issues, toxic exposure concerns, and legal rights.

The site publishes clear, accessible information about how lawsuits work, why certain injuries or exposures become the subject of legal claims, and what issues people often research when trying to better understand their options.

Founded by David Meldofsky, a California-licensed attorney, Lawsuit Informer is built around public legal education rather than legal advice.
