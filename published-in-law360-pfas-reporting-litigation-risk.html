David Meldofsky Published in Law360 on PFAS Reporting and Litigation Risk

Published: April 28, 2026

David Meldofsky, California-licensed attorney and founder of Lawsuit Informer, was published in Law360 with commentary on PFAS reporting developments, compliance uncertainty, documentation, and litigation risk.

Publication note:

The Law360 article includes David Meldofsky’s author bio identifying him as an attorney and the founder of Lawsuit Informer.

The article discusses recent developments involving PFAS reporting and the practical issues that may follow for regulated entities, water systems, affected communities, and litigation stakeholders.

The commentary focuses on how changes in federal reporting and compliance expectations may affect planning, documentation, risk evaluation, and the way PFAS-related issues are discussed in regulatory and litigation settings.

PFAS issues remain important across environmental contamination, drinking water, public health, regulatory compliance, and toxic exposure litigation. Lawsuit Informer continues to cover these topics as part of its broader educational focus on lawsuits, toxic exposure, product liability, and consumer safety issues.
