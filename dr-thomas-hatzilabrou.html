Dr. Thomas A. Hatzilabrou, M.D.

Dr. Thomas A. Hatzilabrou, M.D. serves as a medical reviewer for Lawsuit Informer, helping review health-related content for medical accuracy, sourcing integrity, and clarity.

Medical Reviewer

U.S.-trained physician · Founder of Worldborne Medical

Dr. Hatzilabrou is a U.S.-trained physician with a background in Internal Medicine and specialized clinical rotations in Hematology/Oncology and Neurology.

He is the founder of Worldborne Medical, a physician-led clinical content review practice focused on medical accuracy, sourcing integrity, and YMYL compliance.

His work includes reviewing medical and health-related content for accuracy, clarity, and responsible sourcing in public-facing educational materials.

Background and Review Experience

Dr. Hatzilabrou has peer-reviewed hundreds of international scholarly research articles and has consulted with health and technology organizations on medical review standards and content quality.

His review methodology emphasizes careful sourcing, medically accurate framing, and clear communication for readers seeking understandable health information in legally relevant contexts.

You can also view Dr. Hatzilabrou’s professional profile on LinkedIn.

Review Methodology

On Lawsuit Informer, Dr. Hatzilabrou reviews selected health-related content to help improve medical accuracy, sourcing integrity, and readability. His review methodology emphasizes:

  • Medical accuracy in descriptions of symptoms, diagnoses, and disease processes
  • Clear and responsible use of sources
  • Attention to context in medically sensitive educational content
  • Strong standards for YMYL health information
Important:

Dr. Hatzilabrou’s review supports the medical accuracy of published educational content. His review does not create a doctor-patient relationship and is not a substitute for personal medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
