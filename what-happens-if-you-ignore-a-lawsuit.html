What Happens If You Ignore a Lawsuit?

Ignoring a lawsuit can lead to serious legal consequences. When someone fails to respond to a lawsuit within the required timeframe, the court may proceed without their participation.

Default Judgment

If the defendant does not respond, the court may issue a default judgment in favor of the person who filed the lawsuit.

Financial Consequences

A default judgment may allow the winning party to pursue collection efforts such as wage garnishment or asset seizure depending on the situation.

Loss of Legal Defenses

By ignoring the lawsuit, the defendant loses the opportunity to present evidence or legal defenses in court.

Why Responding Matters

Responding to a lawsuit allows both sides to present their case and ensures the legal process moves forward fairly.
