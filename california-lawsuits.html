Why California Often Matters in Litigation

California often appears in lawsuit-related research because of its large population, broad consumer market, major agricultural activity, industrial operations, environmental concerns, and high level of public attention to consumer safety issues. Readers may begin with a California-specific concern even when the broader lawsuit topic is national in scope.

Many of these issues overlap with broader categories such as Consumer Product Lawsuits, Environmental Contamination Lawsuits, and Product Liability Lawsuits.
