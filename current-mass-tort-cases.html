Current Mass Tort Cases in 2026

By David Meldofsky, California-licensed attorney · Founder, Lawsuit Informer

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Mass tort cases involve large numbers of people bringing similar claims involving the same product, chemical, contaminant, medication, or exposure. These lawsuits often arise from toxic exposure, consumer product safety concerns, environmental contamination, or other product liability issues.

This page provides general educational information about mass tort cases and does not constitute legal advice.

Why These Cases Are Often Called Mass Torts

These types of cases are often described as mass torts because many people may allege similar harm tied to the same product, chemical, or exposure source. Even when the cases are coordinated, each person’s injury history, exposure history, and damages may still be evaluated individually.

Some of these coordinated cases also fit within the broader category of personal injury lawsuits, especially when individuals allege physical injury, illness, or other compensable harm.
