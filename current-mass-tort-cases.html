Current Mass Tort Cases

Mass tort litigation involves large groups of individuals bringing similar claims against companies for injuries caused by defective products, environmental contamination, or toxic exposure. Many of these cases involve allegations that companies failed to warn the public about serious health risks linked to chemicals, pesticides, or consumer products.
