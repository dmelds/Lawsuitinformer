Current Mass Tort Cases

Mass tort litigation involves large groups of individuals bringing similar claims against companies for injuries caused by defective products, environmental contamination, or toxic exposure. This page highlights some current mass tort cases involving chemicals, pesticides, consumer products, and emerging health risks.

Many mass tort lawsuits involve allegations that companies failed to warn the public about known risks or continued selling products linked to serious health concerns.
