Toxic Water Contamination Lawsuits

Toxic water contamination lawsuits arise when harmful chemicals enter drinking water supplies. Communities may be exposed through industrial pollution, firefighting foam, or military base contamination.

Common Causes

  • PFAS chemicals ("forever chemicals")
  • Industrial chemical dumping
  • Military base contamination
  • Landfill runoff
