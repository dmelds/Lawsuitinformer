Illnesses and Exposures Linked to Lawsuits

Some lawsuits involve claims that chemical exposure, environmental contamination, or dangerous products may have contributed to certain illnesses. This page explores common illnesses and exposure topics that are often discussed in litigation.

Cancers Linked to Chemical Exposure

Certain cancers are often researched alongside PFAS contamination, pesticides, talcum powder, and other chemical or product-related exposure claims.

Looking for more specific diagnosis-related topics? Explore cancers linked to lawsuits.

Some product-related claims also involve prenatal exposure and reproductive health issues. Explore reproductive injuries linked to lawsuits.

Kidney Cancer

Some lawsuits involving PFAS contamination and chemical exposure have discussed potential links between exposure and kidney cancer.

Testicular Cancer

PFAS exposure has also been examined in connection with testicular cancer in environmental contamination litigation.

Ovarian Cancer

Some product liability cases involving talc-based products have explored potential links between chemical exposure and ovarian cancer.

Roundup Cancer

Some lawsuits examine whether glyphosate exposure may be associated with certain cancer risks.

Neurological Disorders

Some lawsuits involve claims that pesticide exposure, toxic chemicals, or addictive technologies contributed to neurological disease or behavioral harm.

Parkinson's Disease

Some pesticide lawsuits involve allegations that exposure to paraquat may contribute to Parkinson's disease.

Behavioral Addiction

Some lawsuits involving digital products focus on compulsive use, addictive design, and related behavioral harm.

Environmental Exposure

Some illnesses are researched alongside contaminated drinking water, pesticide exposure, and industrial chemical contamination affecting workers or surrounding communities.

Contaminated Drinking Water

Water contamination lawsuits involve allegations that harmful chemicals entered public or private water systems and exposed communities.

Pesticide Exposure

Agricultural chemical exposure has been discussed in lawsuits involving herbicides and pesticides.

Industrial Chemical Exposure

Some lawsuits involve exposure to chemicals released from manufacturing facilities, industrial sites, or workplace environments.

PFAS Exposure

PFAS contamination lawsuits involve allegations that forever chemicals contaminated water, soil, or nearby communities.
