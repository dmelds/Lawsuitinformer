Editorial Policy

Lawsuit Informer is an independent legal education platform focused on helping individuals understand lawsuits, toxic exposures, and potential legal claims before speaking with a law firm.

The site is created and maintained by David Meldofsky, a licensed attorney, and is designed to provide clear, accessible information based on publicly available legal sources and research.

This page explains how content is developed and the standards used across the site.

Content Development

Content published on Lawsuit Informer is developed to help readers better understand how different types of lawsuits arise, including cases involving consumer products, medications, environmental exposure, and other legal issues.

Articles and legal guides are researched, organized, and edited by David Meldofsky, founder of Lawsuit Informer.

Editorial Standards

Our goal is to present legal topics in a clear, neutral, and educational manner.

Content is based on publicly available legal filings, court records, regulatory information, and scientific research where applicable.

Lawsuit Informer strives to provide accurate and helpful information, though laws, regulations, and litigation developments may change over time.

Educational Purpose

The information published on Lawsuit Informer is intended for general educational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice.

Reading this website or contacting Lawsuit Informer does not create an attorney-client relationship. Individuals seeking legal advice should consult a qualified attorney regarding their specific situation.
