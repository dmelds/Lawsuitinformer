Editorial Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Lawsuit Informer is an attorney-led educational platform focused on helping readers better understand lawsuits, toxic exposures, dangerous products, and related legal issues through clear, structured, and accessible content.

Important:

Content on Lawsuit Informer is provided for general educational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice.

Who Creates the Content

Lawsuit Informer is created and maintained by David Meldofsky, a California-licensed attorney and the founder of the site.

Content is developed to help readers better understand how different types of lawsuits arise, including cases involving consumer products, medications, environmental exposure, toxic substances, and other legal issues people commonly research.

Content Development

Articles and legal guides published on Lawsuit Informer are researched, organized, written, and edited with the goal of making complex legal topics easier to understand.

The site focuses on educational explanations of legal process topics, lawsuit categories, injuries, illnesses, symptoms, and exposure-related issues that readers may be researching before speaking with a lawyer.

Editorial Standards

Lawsuit Informer aims to present legal topics in a clear, neutral, and educational manner.

Content may be developed using publicly available legal materials, court filings, government sources, regulatory information, and other reference materials where appropriate. When health or exposure topics are discussed, the goal is to explain the legal and research context clearly rather than to provide medical advice.

The site strives to provide accurate and useful information, but laws, regulations, scientific understanding, and litigation developments can change over time.

Medical Review of Health-Related Content

Some health-related content on Lawsuit Informer is reviewed for medical accuracy, sourcing integrity, and clarity by Dr. Thomas A. Hatzilabrou, M.D..

Medical review is used to help support the accuracy and responsible presentation of medically sensitive educational content, particularly on pages discussing symptoms, illness, diagnosis, and toxic exposure.

Medical review does not change the educational purpose of the site and does not constitute medical advice or create a doctor-patient relationship.

How Content Is Updated

Pages may be reviewed and updated periodically to improve accuracy, clarity, structure, internal linking, readability, and coverage of developing legal topics.

Because lawsuit activity, legal standards, public records, and scientific understanding can evolve, content may change over time as pages are revised.

Corrections and Reader Feedback

If you believe a page contains an error or needs clarification, you can reach out through the Contact Page. Reader feedback may be used to review and improve existing content.

Educational Purpose

The information published on Lawsuit Informer is intended for general educational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice.

Reading this website, submitting information through a form, or contacting Lawsuit Informer does not create an attorney-client relationship. Individuals seeking legal advice should consult a qualified attorney regarding their own specific situation.

Independence and Reader Focus

The site is designed to help readers better understand the issues they are researching before taking further steps. Content is intended to prioritize clarity, public understanding, and consumer-focused legal education.

The goal is not to overwhelm readers with technical language, but to help them understand the legal topics, risks, and process questions that often arise in connection with potential claims.
