About Lawsuit Informer

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Lawsuit Informer is an attorney-led educational platform focused on lawsuits, toxic exposure, product liability, consumer safety issues, and legal rights, presented in a clear and accessible way.

Important:

Lawsuit Informer provides general educational information only and does not offer legal advice through this website.

Founded by David Meldofsky

California-licensed attorney (CA Bar No. 263673) · Founder of Lawsuit Informer

Lawsuit Informer was founded by David Meldofsky, a California-licensed attorney, to help readers better understand how lawsuit claims, legal research, and next-step decisions often work in practice.

The platform reflects a perspective shaped by legal practice, experience representing injured clients, and practical familiarity with how legal intake, referrals, and claim-evaluation pathways function in the real world. Its purpose is to give readers clearer educational guidance at a moment when legal information can be confusing, stressful, or mixed with advertising and intake prompts.

Our Mission

Lawsuit Informer was created to make legal information easier to understand for people researching potential claims, product risks, environmental exposures, and related legal issues.

Many people begin researching lawsuits during stressful moments, when legal language is unfamiliar and the path forward is not always clear. The goal of this site is to provide practical educational guidance that helps readers better understand how claims are discussed, how legal issues are framed, and what questions to think about before taking further steps.

What We Cover

Lawsuit Informer focuses on topics such as:

  • How lawsuits work and core legal process guides
  • Mass tort and class action topics
  • Toxic exposure and environmental contamination
  • Product liability and consumer safety issues
  • Illnesses, symptoms, and health issues people research alongside lawsuits
  • Common legal research questions related to injuries, exposure, and risk

You can explore more through our Legal Guides, Browse Lawsuits, Illnesses, and Symptoms hubs.

Attorney-Led Legal Education

Lawsuit Informer is led by a California-licensed attorney and built around a clear educational purpose. The site is designed to help readers understand legal topics more clearly before speaking with a lawyer, submitting information, or making decisions about next steps.

Its approach is shaped not only by legal training, but by practical familiarity with how lawsuit claims, legal intake, and claim-screening pathways often work in practice. That perspective helps inform a more transparent, consumer-focused approach to public legal education.

David Meldofsky has also been published in Law360 with commentary on PFAS reporting developments, compliance uncertainty, documentation, and litigation risk.

The purpose of this site is to provide clearer public-facing legal education and guidance, not individualized legal representation through the website itself.

Learn more about David on the author page or connect on LinkedIn.

Editorial Approach

Content on Lawsuit Informer is written to provide general educational information about legal topics in a clear, accessible format. Articles are designed to explain legal concepts accurately while remaining understandable to readers who may not have a legal background.

The site prioritizes clarity, public understanding, and consumer-focused legal education. It is intended to help readers understand the issues they are researching before speaking with a lawyer, submitting information, or taking further steps.

Lawsuit Informer also publishes Professor Perspective, a section featuring academic commentary from law professors and legal academics on legal issues, litigation, and public legal education.

To learn more about how content is created and reviewed, visit the Editorial Policy.

Medical Review

Some health-related content on Lawsuit Informer is reviewed for medical accuracy, sourcing integrity, and clarity by Dr. Thomas A. Hatzilabrou, M.D., a U.S.-trained physician with a background in Internal Medicine and specialized clinical rotations in Hematology/Oncology and Neurology.
