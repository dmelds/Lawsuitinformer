About Lawsuit Informer

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Lawsuit Informer is an attorney-led educational platform focused on explaining lawsuits, toxic exposure risks, consumer safety issues, and legal rights in a clear and accessible way.

Important:

Lawsuit Informer provides general educational information only and does not offer legal advice through this website.

Founded by David Meldofsky

California-licensed attorney · Founder of Lawsuit Informer

Lawsuit Informer was founded by David Meldofsky to make complex legal topics easier to understand through clear, structured educational content.

The platform is designed to help readers better understand how lawsuits work, why certain injuries or exposures become the subject of legal claims, and what issues people often research when exploring their rights and options.

Our Mission

Lawsuit Informer was created to make legal information easier to understand for people researching potential claims, product risks, environmental exposures, and related legal issues.

Many people encounter confusing legal language when trying to understand whether a lawsuit exists, how claims are evaluated, or what rights they may have. The goal of this site is to provide clear, practical educational guidance that helps readers better understand those topics.

What We Cover

Lawsuit Informer focuses on topics such as:

  • How lawsuits work and core legal process guides
  • Mass tort and class action topics
  • Toxic exposure and environmental contamination
  • Product liability and consumer safety issues
  • Illnesses, symptoms, and health issues people research alongside lawsuits
  • Common legal research questions related to injuries, exposure, and risk

You can explore more through our Legal Guides, Browse Lawsuits, Illnesses, and Symptoms hubs.

Attorney-Led Legal Education

Lawsuit Informer is led by a California-licensed attorney and is built around a clear educational purpose. The site focuses on making complex legal topics more transparent and accessible while helping readers better understand the legal and factual issues they may be researching.

Learn more about David on the author page or connect on LinkedIn.

Editorial Approach

Content on Lawsuit Informer is written to provide general educational information about legal topics. Articles are designed to explain legal concepts clearly and accurately while remaining accessible to readers who may not have a legal background.

The site prioritizes clarity, public understanding, and consumer-focused legal education. It is intended to help readers understand the issues they are researching before speaking with a lawyer or taking further steps.

To learn more about how content is created and reviewed, visit the Editorial Policy.

Educational Purpose

The information provided on Lawsuit Informer is for educational and informational purposes only. It is not legal advice, and no attorney-client relationship is formed through the use of this site.

Reading content on this website, submitting information through a form, or contacting the site does not by itself create an attorney-client relationship.

Individuals seeking legal advice should consult a qualified attorney regarding their specific situation.
