Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lawsuit Informer may collect, use, and protect information when you visit the website, submit a form, contact the site, or otherwise interact with its content.

Important:

Lawsuit Informer is an educational platform. Use of this site does not create an attorney-client relationship.

Information We May Collect

Depending on how you interact with the site, Lawsuit Informer may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location information you choose to provide
  • Messages or other information submitted through contact or inquiry forms

The site may also collect certain technical and usage information automatically, such as browser type, device information, pages visited, and general site activity.

How Information May Be Used

Information collected through the site may be used to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Review contact form submissions
  • Improve site content and user experience
  • Monitor site performance and traffic
  • Maintain site security and prevent abuse
  • Communicate about editorial, business, or site-related matters

Analytics and Site Tracking

Lawsuit Informer may use analytics and tracking tools to better understand how visitors use the site. These tools may collect general information about visits, pages viewed, time on site, and similar activity data.

For example, this site uses Clicky analytics to help monitor site traffic and engagement.

Form Submissions

When you submit a form through the site, the information you provide may be stored and forwarded through the site’s form-handling tools or hosting platform so it can be reviewed and answered.

Submitting a form does not by itself create an attorney-client relationship.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Lawsuit Informer and third-party services used on the site may use cookies or similar technologies to support analytics, site functionality, and user experience.

You may be able to manage cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to third-party websites, including social media platforms, external resources, or other informational websites. Lawsuit Informer is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party sites.

How Information Is Protected

Lawsuit Informer takes reasonable steps to protect information submitted through the site, but no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13, and Lawsuit Informer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through the website.

Your Choices

You may choose not to provide personal information through forms or email. You may also manage cookies and related tracking preferences through your browser settings where available.

SMS Communications

If you voluntarily provide a phone number and consent to receive text messages, Lawsuit Informer may use that information to send customer care or follow-up messages related to your inquiry.

Messaging frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

For more information, please review the SMS Terms.

Changes to This Policy

Lawsuit Informer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
